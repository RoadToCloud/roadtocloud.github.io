# The Big Picture
DevOps branching strategies are approaches used in software development to manage and organize code repositories effectively. These strategies dictate how development branches are structured, merged, and deployed, providing a framework for collaboration, version control, and seamless integration in the DevOps lifecycle.  However, it is important to remember that there is no one size fits all approach. Here I discuss four (4) well-known branching strategies, which represent a good summary of standard branching strategies but mutations and customizations of them are possible and frequent.
